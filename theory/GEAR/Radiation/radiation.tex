\begin{abstract}
This is a living logbook for the GEAR subgrid HII photoionization model
(\texttt{src/feedback/GEAR/radiation.c} and related files), branch
\texttt{darwin/gear\_radiation}. It records the algorithm as implemented,
bugs found and fixed, and the ongoing validation against Hu et al.
(2017) and Smith et al. (2021). Edit the numbered \texttt{.tex} files
directly; each covers one topic and is short enough to update on its
own. Regenerate the PDF with \texttt{./run.sh}.
